Delivery

We provide FREE delivery to Klang Valley (Kuala Lumpur & selected areas in Selangor) and selected areas in Penang Island. We deliver to Penang Mainland and selected areas in Johor Bahru with standard delivery charges. For deliveries to Penang Mainland, please order 1 day in advance.

Check out our delivery areas for more info. Alternatively, you can contact us via email at doraflorist@gmail.com.

The flower supply chain slows down over the weekend and public holidays. As we are committed to send you only the freshest, longest lasting flowers, we offer delivery Monday to Saturday for on-demand flowers (same day) and every Thursday for subscription flowers.

ORDER

Yes we do. Our cut-off time for on-demand flowers (same day) delivery is 3.00pm. Therefore, if you place order after 3.00pm today, you will only be able to select tomorrow’s date as the earliest delivery date. We currently only offer same day delivery on weekdays.

For subscription flowers, the cut-off time is at 1.00am on Wednesday. Therefore, if you place an order after 1.00am Wednesday, the flower will be delivered the following Thursday.

For changes on delivery address or message card, you can email us at doraflorist@gmail.com or message us on Facebook or Instagram. Changes to order details can only be made up until one (1) working day before the delivery date.

You may cancel your on-demand order by 10.00am one (1) day before your intended delivery date. A processing fee of RM5 or 5% of the amount paid (whichever is higher) will be imposed on your full refund. Please note that it will take up to 10 working days for the refund process to be completed.

PAYMENT

We accept all major credit cards (Visa & Mastercard) for payment. If you don’t wish to use a credit card, you can also opt for online bank transfer (FPX) via the MolPay payment option during checkout.

We work with Adyen & MolPay to make shopping online safe. Adyen & MolPay holds all credit card information securely. No credit card information is held by us or accessible to us.

MISCELLANEOUS

Our bespoke boxes are especially designed to protect your flowers. We also developed care methods for each individual flower variety within our range hence they are in perfect condition when you receive them. Also check out our blog article on how to keep the flowers fresh.

You can reuse the box for other packaging purposes or you can recycle them.
