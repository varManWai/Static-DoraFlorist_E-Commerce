Free Floral Workshop @ WeWork Creators Market, Plaza Equatorial Kuala Lumpur

Learn how to turn your dried flowers into gorgeous bouquets at the WeWork Creators Market this Saturday 29th June 2019, where BloomThis will have a booth.

From 3-4pm, we have a FREE workshop, where participants will learn how to repurpose cut and dried flowers to create exquisite pieces that can be used to add a touch of beauty to your home.

Workshop registration starts at 2pm at WeWork in Equatorial Plaza. It’s a first come, first served and there are limited spots available, so make sure you’re there on time!

For those who won’t be able to make it for the workshop, no worries, our booth is open from 10am to 5pm.

Come check out some splendid blooms and delicate buds. Talk to our floral designers and see where they get their inspiration for each of our products.

Find out where our flowers come from and what we have in store for the rest of the year.

If you haven’t realised it by now, we’re big on flowers. So if you’re a fan of all things floral, come have a chat with us!
